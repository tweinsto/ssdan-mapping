This site is an easy-to-use tool for exploring social and demographic change in the United States, brought to you by the Social Science Data Analysis Network (SSDAN) at the University of Michigan. With interactive maps and clear visualizations, it covers topics like population growth, age, race and ethnicity, earnings, and poverty. Designed for educators, students, and curious minds alike, the site makes complex data accessible and engaging.
